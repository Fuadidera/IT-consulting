Think! (1-Min Read)


Posted On: Medium


Think! Think!! Think some more!!! There are many distractions, gadgets, and more placing demands on our time that we rarely have time to sit silently and think about our lives, where we are going, what we stand for, what we need, and more. As the world today gets more connected, we lose some of the downtimes and periods of silence. We lose touch with ourselves and our values, and nothing can be more catastrophic. I encourage you to schedule regular periods of silence into your daily schedule. Get to know yourself and your thoughts. Ask yourself, “Why? Why?” and wait for the answer.
